Dayzo - Countdown & Event Tracker

A focused countdown app for birthdays, trips, launches, deadlines, and personal milestones. Dayzo keeps the current purple and orange identity while staying simple: your event titles, dates, and dark mode preference remain on your device.

Built around privacy and simplicity

Dayzo is designed for users who want a beautiful countdown tracker without accounts, remote services, tracking, ads, or unnecessary permissions.

Local storage only

Events are stored on your device using local app storage. Dayzo has no backend, no remote database, no Firebase setup, and no cloud sync.

No account required

Dayzo does not ask you to create a profile, sign in, provide an email address, or connect a social login. Open the app and start tracking dates.

No tracking business model

Dayzo includes no analytics, no advertising networks, no behavioral tracking, no notifications, and no third-party sharing of event data.

Release-ready countdown features

The final Dayzo experience focuses on the expected day-to-day details: reliable persistence, readable dates, helpful empty states, undo for deletion, dark mode, accessibility, and layouts that hold up on smaller screens.

Event persistence

Every event is saved locally and restored when the app opens again. Titles and dates are validated before saving so the countdown list remains clean and useful.

Nearest upcoming sorting

Upcoming events appear first, sorted by the nearest date. Past events move below active countdowns so the next important milestone stays easy to find.

Delete confirmation and undo

Dayzo asks before deleting an event and shows an immediate undo option after confirmation, reducing accidental loss while keeping event management fast.

Accessible and responsive UI

Cards, controls, labels, and countdown summaries are designed for readable text, screen-reader context, touch-friendly actions, and small-screen overflow prevention.

Clear product commitments

Dayzo is intentionally narrow in scope. The app is a countdown and event tracker, not a social platform, ad product, analytics tool, or cloud service.
